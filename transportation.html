How to get around

Amorgos is not a small island and it is totally worth exploring! It is a 90-minute drive from tip to tip with many beautiful beaches, villages and other places of interest in between.

The best way to get around is by car, and we strongly recommend that you rent one. There are car rentals at both ports, Aegiali and Katapola, where you can pick up and drop off a car you've booked in advance. You should be able to pick up your car when your ship arrives even if the car rental is closed - they will just leave your car ready with the keys inside! Most car rentals also rent out scooters and quad bikes as well.

There are several car rentals in Amorgos, some of which are: Thomas , FM , Evdokias , Étoile de Mer , Anemos , and Gryspo . We recommend renting as early as possible. You certainly shouldn't leave it for when you arrive.

You can also get around the island by bus, but service is sporadic and stops early. Also, there are a few taxis on the island, but they operate on a flat rate and are typically very expensive. Sorry guys, no Über in Amorgos yet!
