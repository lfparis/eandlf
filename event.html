The ceremony will take place at the Church of Agia Anna* in the mid-afternoon.
The reception and party will take place afterwards at Vedema bar, located near Aegiali.
Vedema is a 40-minute drive from Agia Anna, and therefore you will need a vehicle to go in-between. Visit our How to get around section for details on how to rent a vehicle on the island.
