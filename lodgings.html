Where to stay

Amorgos has several beautiful villages where you can stay. These are clustered in three areas: the north, the middle and the south.

The north part is centered around the port village of Aegiali, which is within walking distance of several beautiful beaches. Aegiali has several restaurants and bars, including Vedema, where the reception/party will take place.

In the surrounding hills, whithin a short 5-minute driving radius are the three other villages of the area: Tholaria, Langada and Potamos.

We will be staying in the beautiful village of Tholaria. With several bars and restaurants, this charming village has fantastic panoramic views of the surrounding area and sea. Nearby, Potamos also boasts fantastic sea views, but has a very quiet village life. Conversely, Langada offers very little in terms of views but has a much more active restaurant/bar scene.

In the middle part, you can stay either in Chora, which is the capital and biggest village, or in Katapola, the other port of the Amorgos. Chora is considered to be one of the most beautiful villages of the Cycladic Islands. It is very picturesque, with many boutiques and charming restaurants, as well as a great nightlife. However, it does not have any sea views and you must drive to get to the beach.

While Chora is a great place to stay we recommend staying in the north part of Amorgos since the reception/party will take place there. Lastly, we do not recommend staying in the south part of the island since most villages there are very small and don't offer views or an active scene.
